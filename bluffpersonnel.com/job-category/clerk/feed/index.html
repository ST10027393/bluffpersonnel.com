Main Duties include:

-Record business transactions and enter data from daily work logs

-Resolve supplier queries timeously

-Follow up on outstanding invoices and credit notes with suppliers

-Support supervisory/management  staff by performing assigned accounting and clerical tasks.

-Reconcile accounts in a timely manner

-Handle debtors and creditors queries and submit reports with commentary when required

-Compile monthly expense report

-File and maintain an efficient filing system for all documents

-Perform any other duties required by your designated manager

-3+ years relevant working experience in an accounts department handling both debtors and creditors roles

-Computer Literate and experience with Sage Evolution, Excel and Word and Google suite

-Organisation, administrative and communication skills

-Attention to detail, knowledge of accounting rules and legislation

-Ability to explain and interpret fiscal related policies and general accounting.
