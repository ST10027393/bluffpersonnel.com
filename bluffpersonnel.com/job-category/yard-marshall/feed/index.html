To provide transport administration support and service excellence to customers by meeting SLAs to ensure the safe, efficient and effective reconciliation of relevant documentation with specific regard to Transport. To support the safe and incident free loading of product.

Location: Wentworth Terminal, Durban

Main Duties include:

-HSEQ – Communicate, interpret and adhere to HSEQ policies and Zero Tolerance (ZeTo) rules to ensure safety, safeguard business operations and industrial compliance

-Good governance – ensure conformance to the established Limits of Authority (LOA) to safeguard the company’s interest, image and reputation.

-Adherence to policies and provisions of the Codes of Conduct & Business Ethics (COBE), Protection of Personal Information (POPI) and Anti-Bribery & Corruption (ABC)

-Field based, report to the respective shift Transport Operations Supervisor.

-Working closely with the Terminal Operator to direct the flow of vehicles into the Loading Bays in a seamless and incident free manner, whilst adhering to the standard rules and practices within the Wentworth Terminal.

-Supporting the safe and incident free loading of product.

Criteria:

-NQF Level 4 (Matric). 

-Depot or Transport operations experience

-Knowledge of Transport/ Distribution process
