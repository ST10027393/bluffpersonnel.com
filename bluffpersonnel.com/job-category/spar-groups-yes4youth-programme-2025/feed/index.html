SPAR SA’s ongoing commitment to youth empowerment and employment is demonstrated through our implementation of the YES for Youth Programme since 2019, with the aim to create a quality work experience to enable youth to reach their full career potential.

South African Citizen with a Clear Criminal Record
18 – 28 years old, and has Completed and Passed Matric / Gr.12
Currently unemployed and NOT studying full time
Be willing to work retail hours and shifts

Degree Graduates are encouraged to apply.
Trade Qualification in Engineering, Plumbing, Electrical or related
Degree Graduates in Science, Pharmacy, Business, Accounting, HRM, Logistics, SCM, SHEQ, Marketing, Packaging or IT
