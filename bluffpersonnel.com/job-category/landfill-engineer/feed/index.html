Ref: CXP108/2024
Location: KwaDukuza, Stanger
Manage the daily  operations at the landfill sites. Responsible for all phases of landfill work. Management of staff, management of yellow plant machinery and managing community complaints.
Salary: R50 000 – R60 000 TCTC – dependant on experience and qualifications

Main Duties include:

  • Daily operations of the acceptance, treatment & disposal of Hazardous and non-hazardous
    waste.
  • Collating statistics and compiling reports
  • Dealing with enquiries and complaints from members of the public
  • Investigating and following up claims of illegal dumping
  • Evaluate data, prepare proposals and reports for other environmental consulting projects.
  • Provide engineering analyses and calculation
  • Utilize various engineering related computer software
  • Compile project specifications & Complete construction cost estimates
  • Actively manage and track capital budgets for engineering and construction projects and
    report to management and corporate.
  • Ensure projects run smoothly and are completed within budget and on time.
  • Managing consultants, contractors, construction quality assurance firms
  • Preparation of bid documents, bid letting, contract administration, and materials order and
    delivery.
    Criteria:
  • B-Tech/BSC Civil/Rural Engineering qualification
  • 3-5 years’ experience in: Earthworks, Waste / Landfill Management
  • Experience in managing plant and equipment specifically “yellow machines”
  • Excellent knowledge of design and visualizations software such as AutoCAD is essential
  • Knowledge of geosynthetics would be advantageous
  • MUST be able to communicate in isiZulu
  • Good reporting and presentation skills
  • Ability to analyse and interpret data for modelling purposes
  • Knowledge of waste legislation would be an added advantage
  • Prepared to work additional hours as and when required
