Applications are open for a CLO within each of the 5 traditional leadership council areas within Amatikulu. The role of the CLO is to serve their respective traditional council by linking community members to work, training and other opportunities.

Short term contract. Limited positions available!

Criteria:
Matric / Grade 12 passed
Tertiary Qualification is advantageous
At least 1-2 years experience in an NGO environment or community volunteer work
At least 2years computer experience
Interpersonal communication: Cultural sensitivity and ability to collaborate with all stakeholders
Must be fluent in English and another local language
MUST live in the targeted traditional council area
MUST be unemployed

Clear criminal record
